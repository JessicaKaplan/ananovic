LET THE FABRIC DO THE TALK

My handcrafted designs have the origin in the fabric’s texture, pattern, composition and quality. The fine details found on an “Ana Novic” garment are carefully executed until they reach perfection and both you and me are satisfied with the result.

Every piece comes with a story...

I’d like to introduce you to a world of clothes with a story, that match the wearer’s personality and have the power to make you feel special with their unique style and high quality work that I put into them.

“The things that motivate me everyday are the power and the desire to become better, the certainty that I have the talent to make a difference”

The designer that doesn’t draw

You might find it odd, but I prefer to directly cut the material and create what my mind and soul has to say to it. I let myself driven by my creativity, passion and dedication I have for fashion and its’ beauty. Then for the next step, I’ll need you to try it on so we can “sculpt” everything together, for the cloth to reach your exact measurements.

“Everything around me and my work is inspired from nature, from the human body and the dancing world, which I am in love with. I am now using only natural fabrics and my dream for the future is to be 100% sustainable and to spread the love for the nature to all of those around me”

”Not once told my clients that they went to a place dressed in a dress made by me and they were asked if the outfit was conceived by me. I think this is how success looks like for a fashion designer.”

Allow yourself to be yourself

To me, your personality is the best accessory for my creations, and I truly encourage you to show it, by bringing to life the most suitable supporter, the project what makes us both a team..our “Ana Novic” creation
