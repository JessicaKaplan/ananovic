I consider myself an unusual designer because I only create custom made and unique clothes, like pieces of art, as I like to call them. I am happy to say that I experienced the joy of being appreciated and distinguished for this.

Collection 1

In april 2015 I had my debut as Ana Novic, launching my first official collection, "1".

It contained atypical night gowns with original cuts, sexy curves and the original element: manually paintings, a unique thing worldwide.

The paintings were made by my very good friend, Veronica. The Romanian press called our gowns “living paintings”. I still get requests for manually painted dresses from my clients.

Collection 2 title

In november 2016 my second collection, “2” walked the charming rooms of an old storytelling style house in Bucharest.

I was the first one in Romania to have a presentation with only one model,instead of 5-10, like you usually see, a model who dances during the presentation.

Collection “2” is about the infinite power of a well-welded couple, about soulmates, about Yin and Yang and about love in dance steps.

Hemp jackets

I first created a prototype hemp jacket with the occasion of launching collection “1”.

It was firstly worn by a Romanian artist, the singer Alexandra Ungureanu, who attended the event and who was noticed immediately by the local press.

The following hemp jackets continued to surprise, as I set myself a goal for a period of several weeks, meaning that I told myself to create a hemp jacket per week and post it on my social media.

Art meets nature

“Art meets nature” was a blast of colour, dance and emotion, that took fashion, hair styling and make-up to another level.

I am a deep nature lover and I appreciate everything that has to do with it, and most importantly it represents one of my biggest inspiration.

The models represented elements of nature. The decor was astonishing, as our team managed to completely transform an old hall into a floral fantasy themed paradise.
